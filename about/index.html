About

My name is Andrew Bauer. I'm a recent graduate of UC San Diego, where I earned my MS in Computer Science with a focus on physically-based rendering. During my graduate education, I had the opportunity to conduct research on rendering under the guidance of professors Ravi Ramamoorthi and Henrik Wann Jensen. I've also done several internships at companies including Microsoft, Pixar, and Walt Disney Animation Studios.

This site is meant to be a showcase of some personal projects I've worked on, as well as an opportunity to share what I've learned during the process. My own interest in rendering started by reading graphics blogs online, so I hope I can give a little back and inspire others to join this exciting field.
